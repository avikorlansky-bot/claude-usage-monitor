Connect to Claudeone-time

Sign in to claude.ai in any browser, then click below — we'll detect your session automatically. macOS may ask once for permission to read your browser's cookies; click Always Allow.

Enable Safari detection
macOS blocks Safari's cookie file unless this app has Full Disk Access. Click below, drag Claude Usage Monitor.app into the list, toggle it on, then come back and click "Detect my session" again.
Having trouble? Paste manually instead
  1. Open claude.ai in your browser and make sure you're signed in.
  2. Open Developer Tools — ⌘ + Option + I on macOS.
  3. Go to the Application tab (Chrome/Brave/Edge) or Storage tab (Safari/Firefox).
  4. In the left sidebar, expand Cookies → https://claude.ai.
  5. Find the row named sessionKey, double-click its Value, and copy it.
  6. Paste it below and click Connect.
Tip: the value usually starts with sk-ant-sid01-. Don't copy the quotation marks if any.
